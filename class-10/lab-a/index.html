For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy misture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small point over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely luke warm. Whisk in the egg yolks period. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using and ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacture instruction, i.e., let spin and thickne for 20 min before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture and the nuts. Continue process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before servings
